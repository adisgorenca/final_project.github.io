Interstellar Industries

Mission Statment

Interstellar Industries is a research and development organization focused on developing futuristic propulsion systems for space travel. We convert concepts and technology from science fiction to practical, physical devices, allowing mankind to explore the solar system and others nearby.

History

Interstellar Industries was created to restart research in experimental and even theoretical technology. We have been successful at reaching that goal over the past few decades. We have created ion thrusters capable of sending spacecraft throughout the solar system. We have also created prototypes of solar sails, which will be capable of reaching a nearby solar system. We have also signed contracts with SpaceX and Boeing for further development of chemical rockets. We have even received grants from NASA for further research into experimental technology.
